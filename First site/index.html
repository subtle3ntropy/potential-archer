William!

Greetings Netizens! Your average, nearly halfway to thirty years old chinese working class man here. When I got accepted into NYCDA, I didn't know what to expect from the course. What I did expect is the quitting of my kitchen staff job and taking out a bank loan to pay for classes :'( Atleast I feel mighty ambitious and my parents are letting me stay with them without charging rent.

Where I'm from I am recognized on legal documents as a Chinese American. My mother gave birth to me in NYC on December 3rd, 1990. Months after, my parents decided to drop me off with my grandparents in China.
At age five, three strangers; a man, a woman and a little girl visited my family in China to claim ownership of me and take me back with them to the states. To this day, I still do not know if these are my real parents. However I do love them and owe them an enormous debt in raising me. Putting them through retirement is the least I can do.
